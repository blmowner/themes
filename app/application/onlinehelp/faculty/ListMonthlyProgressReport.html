Figure 16: List Monthly Progress Report

Prerequisite

The Supervisor has accepted the invitation

The Student has submitted the Monthly Progress Report

Steps

  1. Click on the Monthly Progress Report tab at the Top Panel section
  2. Click on the List Monthly Progress Report link at the Left Panel section
  3. The List of Student page will be displayed at the Working Area section. Enter the search criteria if to find specific result or otherwise just click the Search button to get general result.
  4. The result will be displayed on the searching result table. Locate the student and click View button to view the detail.
  5. Click Print button if to generate printable view format.
  6. Click Back to Student List button to return back to Student List search page
