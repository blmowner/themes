Figure 44: Online Help

Prerequisite

None



Steps

  1. Click on the HELP tab from the system Top Panel section.
  2. Click on the Online Help on the Left Panel section.
  3. The online help will be displayed on the Working Area section which listed the applicable help to the Student. Click on the online help link.
  4. The Online Help page will be displayed. The student can browse through the content to find the required help.
