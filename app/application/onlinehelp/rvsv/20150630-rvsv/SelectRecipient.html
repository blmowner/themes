Steps:-

  1. Enter or insert Name or Staff ID or Matric No and click Search button.
  2. The system will display the result based on searching criteria.
  3. To select one recipient only, click on the Name displayed.
  4. To select multiple or single recipient, tick on the checkbox on the left.
  5. Then click Select button to proceed.
  6. To go back to the previous page, click on the Black field surrounding the page. Refer Figure 2
