Steps:-

  1. Click Add Attachment button (refer figure 1)
  2. Popup display shown on figure 38 will be shown.
  3. Click Browse button and popup show on figure 2 will be shown and choose necessary attachment to upload. Then click Open button to confirm attachment chosen. (refer figure 2)
  4. Enter file description on the field provided (refer figure 2)
  5. Click Insert Attachment to proceed
  6. Popup show on figure 3 will be shown, click Done button to proceed
