Student Profile listed all the supervisee under supervisor. Plus, the Supervisor can also view all the detail regarding supervisee. Search supervisee by Student Name.

Figure 91: List of Student

Steps:-

  1. Click on the Thesis tab on the system Top panel section.
  2. The Student Profile menu will be displayed on the system Left panel and click on it.
  3. Enter Student Name, then click Search button to view the searching result.
  4. Then click View button to proceed. Refer Student Profile
