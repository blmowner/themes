Thesis Profile listed all the supervisee under supervisor. Plus, the Supervisor can also view all the detail regarding supervisee. Search supervisee by Thesis ID/Title.

Figure 6: Student Programme Tab

Steps:-

  1. Click on the Thesis tab on the system Top panel section.
  2. The Thesis Profile menu will be displayed on the system Left panel and click on it.
  3. Enter Thesis ID or Title, then click Search button to view the searching result.
  4. Then click View button to proceed. Refer Student Profile
