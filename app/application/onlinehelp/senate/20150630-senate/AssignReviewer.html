Steps:-

  1. Choose the department of the reviewer on Department, enter the Staff Name/Staff ID and Field of Expertise and then click the Search button to search.

Figure 35: Proposal Confirmation – Assign Reviewer Search Result

  1. Choose the staff that the user wants to assign by ticking the checkbox.
  2. By default, reply date will be set to 7 days starting the day that been assign.
