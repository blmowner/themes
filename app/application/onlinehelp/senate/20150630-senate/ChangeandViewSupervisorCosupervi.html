Figure 27: Change Supervisor/Co-Supervisor

Steps:-

  1. To assign new Supervisor/Co-supervisor, provide the searching criteria and search. The searching can be by individually or combine.  The fields are as follow:-
    1. Department
    2. Supervisor Name / Staff ID
    3. Field of Expertise / Expertise ID
  2. If there is no entry provided, the search will display all the staff.


Figure 82: Search Result with no searching criteria provided


Figure 29: Search Result with searching criteria provided

  2. Then choose the role of the assigned staff whether to be Supervisor or Co-Supervisor.
  3. Then choose the Reply Date
  4. Once done, click Assign button.
