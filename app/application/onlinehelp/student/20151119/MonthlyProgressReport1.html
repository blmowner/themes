Figure 18: Monthly Progress Report

Pre-condition

  1. After approval by senate
  2. After supervisor/co-supervisor accept invitation
  3. New monthly progress report can be submitted 1 Month after supervisor/co-supervisor accept the invitation

Post-condition

  1. Monthly progress report can be submit
  2. Condition to delete report is the status of the report is “Draft in Progress By Student”.



Steps:-

  1. Click on the Monthly Progress Report tab on the Top panel
  2. Click on the Monthly Progress Report menu on the Left panel
  3. System will display the detail regarding monthly progress report.
  4. To proceed, click New Report button.
  5. To delete, tick on the checkbox on the left side of the table shown above (Tick column) and click Delete Report button.
