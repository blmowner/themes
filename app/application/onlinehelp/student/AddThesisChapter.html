Prerequisite

  1. Senate has approved the thesis proposal
  2. Supervisor / Co-Supervisor has accepted the invitation

Steps

  1. Click on the Monthly Progress Report tab on the Top panel
  2. Click on the Thesis Chapter menu on the Left panel
  3. System will display the thesis chapter page on the Working Area panel.
  4. To add thesis chapter, select chapter number from the Chapter No dropdown field.
  5. Then enter the description of the chapter into the Description field.
  6. Click Add Chapter button to save the chapter.


Next Action

The student can proceed with the creation of thesis sub-chapter or submission of the monthly progress report to the Supervisor.

Warning

System will prompt a notification message if the addition of the thesis chapter without selecting the chapter number and providing its description.
