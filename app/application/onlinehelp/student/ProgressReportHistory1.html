Progress Report History

Getting Started - Student ›› MONTHLY PROGRESS REPORT ››

This feature will allow the user to view the history progress of the monthly report starting from its submission until approval stage.

Figure 32: Progress Report History

Prerequisite

At least one monthly progress report has been submitted by the student.

Steps

  1. Click on the Monthly Progress Report at the Top Panel.
  2. Click on the Monthly Progress Report at the Left Panel
  3. By default the system will show no history records on the Working Area section.
  4. You can list the record by providing the search criteria or just click the Search button.
  5. The search result will be displayed accordingly.
  6. Click on the Thesis ID link if to view the thesis detail.
  7. Click on the Reference No link if to view the monthly progress report detail.
