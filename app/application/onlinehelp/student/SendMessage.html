Send Message

Figure 47: New Message

Prerequisite

None


Steps

  1. Click on the Message tab on the Top panel
  2. Click on the New Message menu on the Left panel
  3. Once the Recipient, the message and the attachment (if any) are in place, then click Send button to send it to the Recipient.


Next Action

None


Warning
