Thesis History

Getting Started - Student ›› THESIS ›› Student Programme ››

Thesis History tab contains the proposal progress history starting from its submission by the student tills its approval by the Senate.


Figure 21: Student Programme – Thesis History


Prerequisite

At least the Student has submitted the thesis proposal to the Faculty.


Steps

  1. Click on the Thesis tab on the Top panel
  2. Click on the Student Programme menu on the Left panel
  3. Click on the Thesis History tab on the Working Area
  4. System will display the list of the thesis history starting from its submission till its approval.
